What is a Coffee Rave?

A Coffee Rave is the fusion of techno/rave culture and the chill, welcoming vibe of a café.

  • High-energy music experience
  • Morning or evening vibe
  • Customers can still order as usual
  • Lights, DJ & community-driven atmosphere
  • Built for content, connection, and culture

Who Are We?

We are RoastnRave – a next-gen experiential brand.

  • Mission: Turn everyday cafés into unforgettable hotspots
  • Instagram-first, vibe-focused
  • Run by passionate creatives & music lovers
  • Building a loyal, socially-active community
  • Collaborating with local DJs, artists & sponsors

Our Model

We don’t charge the café. We don’t charge the audience. Everybody wins.

  • Café = Free exposure, footfall, and sales
  • Audience = Free entry, good vibes
  • Sponsors = Get branding & visibility
  • RoastnRave = Grows social presence & community

What We Bring

  • Portable DJ setup & sound system
  • Lighting (non-intrusive)
  • Social media promotion
  • Professional content capture
  • Creative concept design & execution

We handle everything. You focus on what you do best: serving great food & drinks.

What We Need From You

  • 2–3 hours of your space (morning/evening slot)
  • Basic electricity access
  • Permission to film content
  • Collaboration spirit

Why This Works

  • Cafés: More footfall = more sales = more buzz
  • DJs: Live gigs = exposure + content
  • Audience: Free experience = share-worthy memories
  • Sponsors: Brand placement in highly social events

This is a community-first movement built on vibes and value.
